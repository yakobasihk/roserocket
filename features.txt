Features:

REST API: You can get /stops for all the stops, /legs for all the legs and /driver for driver location. 
2 Time functions: Time to finish leg which calculates how long it will take the driver to complete the current leg,
                  Time to finish all legs which will calculate how long it will take the driver to finish the entire route.
Distance Remaining: Show how many kilometers the drivers has yet to drive to finish the route.

The Stops are all marked by their letter name on the canvas. 
The green yellowish-green line indicates the progress on the current leg where the driver is.
The driver is indicated by a green circle.
The black line indicates the past legs that have been completed.
The green line indicates the legs that have yet to be completed.

Form change allows the user to change where the driver is based on a percentage of the selected leg.
